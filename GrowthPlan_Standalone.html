Guide & Important Notes

🚀 3 Steps to Plan

  • Step 1: Discover (🧭): Define your career vision, strengths, and growth areas.
  • Step 2: Find Gaps (📈): Evaluate your current skills against your target role's requirements.
  • Step 3: Bridge Gaps (🎯): Set actionable goals and enroll in necessary courses.

🔒 100% Data Security

This application runs entirely in your browser using localStorage.

Your data is completely private. It is only saved on your current device and is never sent to any server or database over the internet.

Note: If you open this exact link on another device, it will show a blank canvas. Everyone has their own isolated workspace!

💾 How to Never Lose Data

If you clear your browser history/cache or switch devices, your data will be gone.

The Solution:

  • Click Backup Plan (bottom left) to download your data as a .json file.
  • Click Restore Plan on any device to upload the file and continue right where you left off!
